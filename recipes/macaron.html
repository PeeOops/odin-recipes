Macaron

Description

Macarons are small, round, meringue-based cookies that are sandwiched together with a filling. They are known for their delicate shells, smooth texture, and variety of flavors.

Ingredients

  • Almond Flour
  • Egg White
  • Icing Sugar
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Food Coloring

Steps

  1. Whisk Egg White
  2. Add in both types of sugar and food coloring along the way
  3. Bake in the oven
