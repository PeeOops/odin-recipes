Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian pasta dish made of flat, wide sheets of pasta layered with fillings and baked. The dish is typically made with tomato sauce, cheese, and sometimes meat.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Fillings
  • Sauces
  • Meats
  • Cheeses

Steps

  1. Assemble the pasta with other ingredients
  2. Bake in an oven
  3. Cut into single serving
